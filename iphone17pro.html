Chapter 1: Design Philosophy

The iPhone 17 Pro is Apple's premium smartphone designed for users who want professional performance, advanced photography, and a compact flagship experience.

The phone is made up of hundreds of precision electronic components, including:

  • Display
  • Motherboard (Logic Board)
  • Processor (Chipset)
  • Memory
  • Cameras
  • Battery
  • Cooling System
  • Antennas
  • Sensors

All of these components work together as a single integrated system.

Chapter 2: Exterior Design

The titanium frame provides strength while keeping the device lightweight. The exterior protects the internal hardware, improves heat dissipation, and supports wireless connectivity.

  • Titanium frame
  • Front glass
  • Rear glass
  • Action Button
  • Camera Control
  • USB-C port
  • Speakers
  • Microphones
  • Camera lenses

Every opening on the phone serves an important function, from microphones to antennas.

Chapter 3: The Display

The iPhone 17 Pro features a 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion technology up to 120Hz.

  • Ceramic Shield glass
  • Oleophobic coating
  • Touch layer
  • OLED panel
  • Display controller
  • Flex connectors

How OLED Works

Each pixel emits its own light, delivering perfect blacks, high contrast, and improved power efficiency.
