Chapter 1: Design Philosophy

The iPhone 17 Pro Max is Apple's most advanced smartphone in the iPhone lineup. It is designed for users who want the very best in performance, photography, display quality, and battery life.

The phone is made up of hundreds of precision electronic components, including:

  • Display
  • Motherboard (Logic Board)
  • Processor (Chipset)
  • Memory
  • Cameras
  • Battery
  • Cooling System
  • Antennas
  • Sensors

All of these components work together as a single integrated system.

Chapter 2: Exterior Design

The exterior is more than just a protective shell. It safeguards the internal components, helps dissipate heat, and improves wireless signal reception.

  • Metal frame
  • Front glass
  • Rear glass
  • Side buttons
  • USB-C port
  • Speakers
  • Microphones
  • Camera lenses

Even small openings contain important hardware like microphones or speakers.

Chapter 3: The Display

The display is one of the most expensive components of the phone, second only to the processor.

  • Protective glass
  • Fingerprint-resistant coating
  • Touch layer
  • OLED panel
  • Control layer
  • Flex connectors

How OLED Works

Each pixel produces its own light. Black pixels turn off completely, saving energy and increasing contrast.
